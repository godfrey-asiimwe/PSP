Perspectives


Perspectives is a global movement awakening Christians all over the world to pursue and fulfill their role in God’s global purpose of glorifying Himself among all peoples.

The Perspectives course has been changing lives since it originated in 1974 in the US and the movement has spread out to many countries since. The course was first introduced in Uganda in 2017 and is currently taught in two cities in the country, offering Christians a deep and broader perspective on the Church and its mission.

Perspectives Uganda is facilitated by the PT Institute and mobilized by ACEMU.

What is Perspectives

Perspectives is a dynamic 15-week missional course designed to help students explore some of the big questions of life.

What is your spiritual identity in Christ? What are we here for? What are we saved for? What purpose, what work, and what mission does God have for you? How can you be a part of what God is doing, locally or globally?

The course is designed to help believers learn more about God's heart for all nations and how believers worldwide can play an active role in the Great Commission through equipping the Body of Christ for strategic participation in His plan to reach all peoples, tribes, tongues, and nations. It’s an effort to help the Body of Christ see the world and their place in it like Jesus does.

Why Perspectives?

God has a "world-sized" role for every Christian in His global purpose. The Perspectives Course is a journey to discover that purpose which places every Christian in God’s grand narrative and mission. Believers from all walks of life will be threaded into the biggest story of all time to see God’s heart for all tribes, tongues, peoples, and nations.

The primary issue is what most people are hungry to discover: vision to live a life of purpose. Discovering that vision makes this course valuable, and perhaps crucial, for any Christian.

Our desire is to see the Christians in Uganda intentionally living with that same passion, hope, joy, and purpose that Christ did and the Church in Uganda passionately and strategically carrying out its role in God’s mission for His Glory.

How does it work?

Perspectives is designed around four vantage points or "perspectives" — Biblical, Historical, Cultural, and Strategic with each one highlighting different aspects of God's global purpose.

Biblical – The Word of God

With striking clarity, Perspectives illuminates God’s unchanging promises as they unfold from Genesis to Revelation. You may never read your Bible in the same way when you experience how Jesus threads believers like yourself into His epic story.

Historical – Your Spiritual Lineage

Christians’ history is wrought with the immeasurable power of God. From Abraham to today, you will walk in the shoes of believers who have paved the way and set the stage to thread us all into God’s story.

Cultural – Peoples of the World

Through Christ Jesus, we will eventually see the expression of the New Testament Church thriving among every nation, tribe, people, and language. You will experience how God is threading diverse peoples into His Story in culturally relevant ways.

Strategic – Your place in the Story

There are endless opportunities to help complete the Great Commission. Getting threaded into the story is about discovering practical and strategic opportunities to leverage your passions, training, and expertise for His Glory, both at home and abroad.

Who it is for?

Perspectives on the World Christian Movement is a Christian education course. In Perspectives, you will experience God’s heart for all peoples and encounter the momentum of the World Christian Movement. It will open the eyes of your heart with fresh knowledge and understanding of God’s unchanging purposes and why they’re relevant to your life.

All Christians are encouraged to take up the course!
